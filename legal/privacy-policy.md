# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 22, 2026  
**Effective Date**: January 22, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Vannilli ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our music video generation service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, password (hashed), payment information
- **Profile Data**: Username, display name (optional)
- **Content**: Performance videos, images, audio files, prompts
- **Payment Information**: Processed by Stripe (we store only Stripe customer ID)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, generation history
- **Device Information**: Device fingerprint, IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Technical Data**: Session duration, error logs, performance metrics
- **Cookies**: Session cookies, preference cookies (see Section 8)

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Stripe**: Payment confirmation, subscription status
- **AI video generation provider**: Video generation status (we do not share personal information beyond what's necessary for processing)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 To Provide the Service
- Process video generation requests
- Manage your account and subscriptions
- Deliver customer support
- Send transactional emails (receipts, generation status)

### 3.2 To Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features
- Conduct A/B testing

### 3.3 For Security and Fraud Prevention
- Detect and prevent fraud (device fingerprinting)
- Enforce Terms of Service
- Protect against unauthorized access

### 3.4 For Marketing (With Consent)
- Send promotional emails (you can opt-out)
- Display personalized content
- Run referral programs

### 3.5 For Compliance
- Respond to legal requests
- Enforce our rights
- Comply with regulations (GDPR, CCPA, COPPA)

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data based on:
- **Contract**: Necessary to provide the Service
- **Legitimate Interest**: Improve the Service, prevent fraud
- **Consent**: Marketing communications, optional features
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with laws

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Service Providers
We share data with:
- **Cloudflare**: Hosting, CDN, storage (R2)
- **Supabase**: Database, authentication
- **Stripe**: Payment processing
- **AI video generation provider**: Video generation (performance video, target image)
- **Sentry**: Error monitoring
- **Mixpanel**: Analytics (anonymized)

### 5.2 Business Transfers
If we are acquired or merged, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

### 5.4 With Your Consent
- Showcase gallery (you opt-in)
- Referral program participants

### 5.5 We Never Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Active Accounts
We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

### 6.2 Uploaded Content
- **Raw uploads** (performance videos, images): Deleted after 24 hours
- **Generated videos**: Stored for 30 days, then deleted
- **Showcase videos**: Retained until you opt-out

### 6.3 Account Data
After account deletion, we retain:
- Transaction records: 7 years (for tax compliance)
- Audit logs: 90 days
- All other data: Deleted within 30 days

### 6.4 Backups
Backup copies may persist for up to 30 additional days.
